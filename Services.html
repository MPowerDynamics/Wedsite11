Services

Service Agreements and Diagnostics & Repairs

Services

At MPower Dynamics, we ensure your power systems perform reliably when you need them most. Our service solutions are designed to maintain your generator peak performance, extend equipment lifespan, and comply with the latest CSA C282 standards for emergency power systems following the manufacturer recommendations and instruction manuals. We provide expert support through two main programs: Service Agreements and Diagnostics & Repairs.

Service Agreements

Our Service Agreements are designed to provide peace of mind and uninterrupted operation through scheduled maintenance and proactive system checks. Each plan is customized to your generator type, usage, and operational needs, ensuring compliance with CSA-C282 standards for example Clauses 11.1.2 to 11.5, a long with the manufacturer recommendations.

Preventive Maintenance Options:

  • Weekly Inspections: Quick visual checks for fluid levels, leaks, battery condition, and overall system readiness.

  • Monthly Services: Performance verification, building load testing, and system checks by certified technicians.

  • Semi-Annual Services: In-depth mechanical and electrical inspections, protective device testing, and governor calibration.

  • Annual Services: Complete oil, fuel, and filter replacement, load bank testing, and thermal imaging of critical connections, comple.

  • Five-Year (Quinquennial) Service: Full system overhaul including coolant replacement, alternator insulation testing, valve adjustments, and advanced diagnostics.

All services are performed by trained technicians following CSA-C282 Emergency Electrical Power Supply for Buildings guidelines. Regular maintenance ensures your generator operates safely, efficiently, and remains compliant with regulatory standards.

Key Benefits:

  • Reduced downtime and costly

  • Documented CSA compliance and service logs.

  • Priority emergency support for contract customers.

  • Extended equipment life and operational reliability.

Diagnostics & Repairs

Even the most reliable systems require expert attention from time to time. MPower Dynamics offers comprehensive diagnostic and repair services to identify and resolve performance issues quickly and effectively.

Our Capabilities Include:

  • Full electrical and mechanical diagnostics using advanced testing tools.

  • Repair and replacement of control components, alternators, and fuel systems.

  • Troubleshooting for automatic transfer switches (ATS) and control panels.

  • Load bank testing and real-time performance verification.

  • Emergency repair response available 24/7 for contract clients.

Our technicians combine hands-on expertise with industry-standard testing equipment to ensure every repair is completed with precision and safety. Whether you operate a residential, commercial, or industrial generator system, we provide tailored solutions that restore reliability and confidence in your backup power.
